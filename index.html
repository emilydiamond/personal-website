Emily Diamond

Emily Diamond is currently the Product Director at Quartz where she draws from her diverse experiences in media, business, and product development in order to drive company alignment and strategy. Her work is centered around unifying Quartz's mission and innovating across verticals—editorial, sales, marketing, design, and engineering—to deliver on the brand’s creative approach to global business news.

She has held several different roles at Quartz, including Product Manager of QZ.com Product Manager of Advertising and Business Operations, and Director of Planning on the business team. Prior to her tenure at Quartz, she worked in digital sales planning at Lucky Magazine (RIP) and GQ. Emily has a dual degree in Marketing and Organization & Management from Emory University’s Goizueta Business School.

Emily was recently accepted into the 2020 cohort of the ONA Women's Leadership Accelerator.

Emily has a passion for skiing, celebrity-based crossword puzzles, and hot lunch buffets. She is also trying her hand at coding with this very website.
